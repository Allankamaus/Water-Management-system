About Water System

Water System is a simulated customer portal designed to help households and businesses manage billing, report service issues, and receive official notices from the utility provider in one place.

Our operations team monitors service continuity across all zones and responds to complaints, outage reports, and connection requests with the goal of delivering reliable, safe, and affordable water services.

Mission

To provide dependable access to clean water through transparent account management, timely support, and proactive maintenance communication.

Coverage Areas (Simulated)

Zone 1: Riverside, Market Ward, and Hillview
Zone 2: Central Business District and West End
Zone 3: Greenfields and Upper Plains
Zone 4: East Gate and Industrial Park
Zone 5: Lakeside and New Estate

Service Standards (Simulated)

Complaint acknowledgement within 2 hours
Routine outage notices posted at least 24 hours before planned maintenance
Connection requests reviewed within 3 business days
Billing support response within 1 business day
